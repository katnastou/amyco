ID: 52

Disease Name: Inflammatory Bowel Diseases

Description: Chronic, non-specific inflammation of the gastrointestinal tract. Etiology may be genetic or environmental. This term includes Crohn disease and Ulcerative colitis.

Disease Type: Clinical conditions associated with amyloidosis

ICD-10 Classification: Diseases of the Digestive System

Tissue: Liver, Gastrointestinal Tract
