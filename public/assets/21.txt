ID: 21

Disease Name: Thyroid Cancer, Medullary

Description: A neuroendocrine carcinoma that originates in the c-cells of the thyroid gland; about 10-20% of cases are familial. It occurs sporadically or is associated with multiple endocrine neoplasia syndromes. Germline and somatic mutations in RET and NTRK1 genes have been identified.

Disease Type: Amyloidosis

ICD-10 Classification: Neoplasms

Tissue: C-cell thyroid tumors
