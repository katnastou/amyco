ID: 15

Disease Name: Prolactinoma

Description: A pituitary adenoma which secretes prolactin, leading to hyperprolactinemia. Clinical manifestations include amenorrhea; galactorrhea; impotence; headache; visual disturbances; and cerebrospinal fluid rhinorrhea.

Disease Type: Amyloidosis

ICD-10 Classification: Neoplasms

Tissue: Pituitary Gland
