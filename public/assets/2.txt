ID: 2

Disease Name: Diabetes Mellitus, Type 2

Description: A subclass of diabetes mellitus that is not insulin-responsive or dependent (NIDDM). It is characterized initially by insulin resistance and hyperinsulinemia; and eventually by glucose intolerance; hyperglycemia; and overt diabetes. Type II diabetes mellitus is no longer considered a disease exclusively found in adults. Patients seldom develop ketosis but often exhibit obesity.

Disease Type: Amyloidosis

ICD-10 Classification: Endocrine, Nutritional and Metabolic Diseases

Tissue: Pancreas (Islets of Langerhans)
