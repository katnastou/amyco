ID: 48

Disease Name: Keratosis, Seborrheic

Description: Benign eccrine poromas that present as multiple oval, brown-to-black plaques, located mostly on the chest and back. the age of onset is usually in the fourth or fifth decade.

Disease Type: Clinical conditions associated with amyloidosis

ICD-10 Classification: Diseases of the Skin and Subcutaneous Tissue

Tissue: Skin
