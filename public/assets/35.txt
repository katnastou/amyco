ID: 35

Disease Name: Calcifying Epithelial Odontogenic Tumor

Description: A rare slow growing, locally invasive odontogenic tumor. It most often grows intraosseously in the mandible and less frequently in the maxilla, and gingiva. It is characterized by the presence of a fibrous stroma, epithelial cells with abundant eosinophilic cytoplasm, and amyloid material which is often calcified.

Disease Type: Amyloidosis

ICD-10 Classification: Neoplasms

Tissue: Mandible, Maxilla, Gingiva
