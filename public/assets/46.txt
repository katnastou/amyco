ID: 46

Disease Name: Lymphoma, Non-Hodgkin

Description: Any of a group of malignant tumors of lymphoid tissue that differ from Hodgkin disease, being more heterogeneous with respect to malignant cell lineage, clinical course, prognosis, and therapy. The only common feature among these tumors is the absence of giant Reed-Sternberg cells, a characteristic of Hodgkin's disease.

Disease Type: Clinical conditions associated with amyloidosis

ICD-10 Classification: Neoplasms

Tissue: Heart, Kidney, Liver, Bone Marrow, Lymph Node, Spleen
